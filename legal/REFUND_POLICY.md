# Refund Policy
*Bunche — Automated Proxy Retail Service*
*Effective Date: 2026-07-01*

---

## 1. Overview

Bunche offers a 24-hour refund window for qualifying orders. This policy explains what qualifies for a refund, how to request one, and how long it takes.

---

## 2. Refund Window

You may request a refund within **24 hours** of receiving your proxy credentials.

The 24-hour window starts when:
- Website orders: credentials are displayed on the thank-you page
- Chat orders: credentials are delivered in Telegram or WhatsApp

After 24 hours, refunds are not available except in exceptional circumstances at Bunche's discretion.

---

## 3. Qualifying for a Refund

A refund is available if **both** conditions are met:

**Condition 1:** The proxy IP did not work at the time of delivery.
- "Did not work" means: the IP was offline, unreachable, or returned an authentication error when used correctly with the provided credentials.
- An IP being blocked by your target website or service is **not** a refund matter — see Section 5 (Ban Claims vs. Refunds).

**Condition 2:** You request within 24 hours of delivery.

### 3.1 What We Consider

When evaluating a refund request, we consider:
- Whether the proxy was functional at the time of delivery (we log credential issuance timestamps)
- Whether the customer attempted to use the proxy correctly
- Whether the reported issue could be caused by the customer's configuration, target website, or network

### 3.2 What We Do Not Refund

We do not refund:
- Requests made more than 24 hours after delivery
- IPs blocked by your target website or service (see Section 5)
- Customer change of mind after the 24-hour window
- Proxies that stopped working due to customer's misuse or misconfiguration
- Data plans where more than 10% of the data quota has been used
- Proxies used for prohibited activities under our Acceptable Use Policy
- Orders that were fulfilled correctly and were functional at delivery

3. Select "Request a Refund"
4. Provide a brief description of why the proxy did not work
5. Submit

### 4.2 Via Chat

### 4.3 Via Email
Send an email to hello@styxproxy.com with:
- Your order number (tx_ref)
- A description of why the proxy did not work
- Any screenshots or evidence

---

## 5. Ban Claims vs. Refunds

These are two different things. Using the wrong process delays resolution.

| Situation | What to do |
|-----------|------------|
| Proxy IP was offline at delivery or returned auth error | Request a **refund** |
| Proxy IP worked at delivery but is now blocked by a website | Raise a **ban claim** |
| You changed your mind after 24 hours | Not eligible for refund or ban claim |

Ban claims do not result in refunds. Ban claims result in replacement credentials if the claim is verified.

---

## 6. Refund Processing

### 6.1 Review

All refund requests are reviewed manually. We aim to review requests within 2 business hours during business hours (9am–6pm WAT, Monday–Saturday).

During review, we check:
- The timestamp of credential delivery
- The status of the IP at delivery time (from our logs)
- Whether the reported issue is a legitimate delivery failure

### 6.2 Approval

If approved, refunds are processed within **5–10 business days** to your original payment method.

Flutterwave processes refunds back to the card or account you used to pay. The refund will appear as "Bunche" or "Flutterwave" on your statement.

### 6.3 Rejection

If your refund is rejected, we will tell you why. Common reasons for rejection:
- "The proxy worked at delivery time" — submit a ban claim instead
- "Request made more than 24 hours after delivery" — outside the refund window
- "More than 10% of data quota used" — data plan partially consumed
