# Privacy Policy
*Bunche — Automated Proxy Retail Service*
*Effective Date: [DATE]*

---

## Introduction

Bunche ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our WhatsApp-based proxy ordering service.

This policy complies with:
- Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Phone number (primary identifier)
- Name (optional)
- Email address (if you contact us via email)
- Order history and communication preferences

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- IP addresses (when you interact with our webhook endpoints)
- Device information (browser type, OS)
- n8n workflow execution logs
- Proxy usage volume

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Flutterwave:** Transaction status, payment confirmation
- **Meta / WhatsApp:** Message metadata via WhatsApp Business API
- **Proxy Providers:** Proxy performance data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Process and fulfill orders | Phone number, order details |
| Deliver proxy credentials | WhatsApp number |
| Send renewal reminders | Phone number, order history |
| Handle refunds | Phone number, transaction info |
| Fraud prevention | Payment data, usage patterns |

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Flutterwave:** For payment processing
- **Meta / WhatsApp:** For message delivery
- **Proxy Providers:** For proxy allocation
- **Google Sheets:** For order database
- **n8n:** For workflow processing

---

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|----------------|
| Order history | 7 years |
| Customer contact info | Until deletion or 2 years of inactivity |
| Payment records | 7 years |
| WhatsApp messages | 90 days |
| n8n execution logs | 30 days |

---

## 5. Your Rights

Under NDPR (Nigeria), you have the right to:
- Know what personal data we hold about you
- Request access to your data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)

To exercise your rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email.

---

## 6. Data Security

- **Encryption:** All data in transit uses TLS 1.2+
- **Access Controls:** n8n and Google Sheets access is restricted to authorized personnel only
- **API Security:** All API keys stored securely in n8n credentials
