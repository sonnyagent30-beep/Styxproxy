UX Brief v2.0

Overview

Styxproxy is a global anonymous proxy service with a dark, cryptic brand identity rooted in Greek mythology — the River Styx as the gateway to anonymity. The design should feel like crossing into a hidden realm: serious, professional, fast, and slightly ominous.

Brand Voice Professional Dark/Cryptic Global
