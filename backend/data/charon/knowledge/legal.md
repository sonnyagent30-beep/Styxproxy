## What data we collect

For website orders: almost nothing. The only identifier is a small file
stored on the customer's own device that links their purchase history
to their browser. Clearing site data removes the link.

For Telegram and WhatsApp orders: the chat ID, messages, and any
delivery information the customer provides. Customers can request
deletion of this data at any time by sending "delete my data" to the
Telegram bot or emailing privacy@styxproxy.com.

## What we never do

- Never store cookies on customer browsers.
- Never use third-party tracking pixels or analytics that
  fingerprint the visitor.
- Never share customer data with anyone except a payment processor
  necessary to process the order, and law enforcement only when
  legally compelled.

## Acceptable use

The proxy cannot be used for:

- Anything illegal under the customer's jurisdiction or ours.
- Email spam, phishing, or mass unsolicited messaging.
- Hacking, fraud, or unauthorized access to systems.
- Child exploitation material.
- Anything that violates another party's terms of service in a way
  that puts Styxproxy at legal risk.

The full list is on the Acceptable Use page.

## Payment processor

All payments are processed by a third-party payment processor (Flutterwave).
Styxproxy never sees the customer's card number or full bank details,
only the transaction reference and payment status.

For refund requests, the customer should email support@styxproxy.com
with their transaction reference. Refund requests are reviewed by a human.

## Disputes and complaints

If a customer has a complaint that Charon cannot resolve, they can:

- Email support@styxproxy.com
- Use the contact form at styxproxy.com/contact

For privacy-related requests: privacy@styxproxy.com.

For abuse reports from third parties (security researchers, platforms
seeing malicious traffic): abuse@styxproxy.com.

For legal process: legal@styxproxy.com.
