# Charon Knowledge Base — FAQ

## About Styxproxy

Styxproxy is an anonymous proxy service. Customers can buy proxies in
four types — ISP, Residential, Mobile 4G, and Datacenter — across more than
20 countries. The service is fully anonymous. Website orders require no
account, no email, and no phone number.

The brand and the chatbot are both named after characters in Greek
mythology. Styxproxy is the river that separates the world of the living
from the underworld. Charon is the ferryman who takes payments across.

## Delivery time

After payment is confirmed:

- Card / USSD / QR payments: credentials are ready in minutes.
- Bank transfer: a few minutes longer while the bank settles.

The credentials appear on the confirmation page and can also be looked up
later from the Manage page by transaction reference.

## Order tracking

Customers can look up their order by transaction reference on the
Manage page. The page shows credentials, plan, expiry, and an option
to request a fresh IP or report a banned IP.

## Replacement policy

If a proxy IP is dead on arrival or banned shortly after delivery, customers
can request a free replacement from the Manage page using their
transaction reference. Replacements rotate the upstream IP to a new one
that is verified before being shown.

## Payment methods

We accept:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Bank transfer (Nigeria: Access, UBA, GTBank, and others)
- USSD (Nigerian cards)
- QR code payment

Crypto is not accepted. There is no anonymity advantage in crypto for
purchases — the public ledger defeats the purpose of buying an
anonymity tool.

## Refunds

A full refund is available within a short window of receiving
credentials if the proxy does not work or if payment was made but the
proxy was never delivered. Refunds for delivery failures are automatic.

For full terms, see the Refund Policy on styxproxy.com/refund-policy.

## Account and personal data

Website orders require no account. No email, no phone number. The only
identifier that links a purchase to a browser is a small file stored on
the customer's own device. Clearing site data removes that link.

To request deletion of any data stored from Telegram or WhatsApp
interactions, the customer can send "delete my data" to the Telegram
bot, or email privacy@styxproxy.com.

## How proxies protect your privacy

When a customer connects through a Styxproxy server, their real IP
address is hidden. The destination only sees the proxy IP.

For maximum anonymity:

- Residential proxies carry a real ISP-assigned IP from a home network
- Mobile proxies carry an IP from a mobile carrier
- These look like normal household traffic, not datacenter traffic

Streaming services, social platforms, and most other services have
much lower friction with residential or mobile proxies.

## When to escalate to a human

Charon handles most questions. When a customer wants:

- A refund the customer has to escalate
- A rotation or replacement
- To dispute a charge
- Anything that needs legal sign-off

then Charon connects them to the contact form and someone on the
team replies by email.

For credential questions, Charon uses the order-lookup tool first.
For payment issues, Charon uses the payment-status tool.

## What Charon cannot do

To be transparent about limits:

- Charon cannot rotate IPs directly without a customer-initiated
  replacement request from the Manage page.
- Charon cannot issue refunds directly — refunds are reviewed by a human.
- Charon cannot delete another customer's data.
- Charon cannot change prices or product offerings.
- Charon cannot write to the database except via approved tools.

When a customer asks for something Charon cannot do, the answer
should be honest: "I cannot do that, but here is how to escalate it."
