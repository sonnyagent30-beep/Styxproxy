# Charon Knowledge Base — Products

## ISP Proxies

ISP proxies are IPs assigned by real Internet Service Providers but
hosted on our infrastructure. They look like residential or office
connections to most services, while still being fast enough for high-
throughput workloads.

Best for: web scraping, automation, account creation, sneaker sites.
Starting price: from ₦6,500 per month.

Available in: United Kingdom, United States, Germany, France,
Canada, Japan, Australia, Brazil, Singapore. More regions available on
request.

## Residential Proxies

Residential proxies are real home IPs assigned to real subscribers.
They are the hardest to detect and block for most platforms.

Best for: social media management, ad verification, market research,
sneaker bots.

Pricing is per gigabyte of traffic. Plans:

- 5 GB — ₦5,000
- 10 GB — ₦20,000
- 50 GB — ₦80,000

Available in 14 countries.

## Mobile 4G Proxies

Mobile proxies come from real mobile carrier networks. Mobile IPs
score highest in trust signals for platforms because real carrier

Best for: account creation, social platforms that fingerprint mobile
traffic highly, ad verification.

Pricing is per gigabyte of traffic. Plans:

- 5 GB — ₦25,000
- 10 GB — ₦45,000

Available in 12 countries.

## Datacenter Proxies

Datacenter proxies are hosted on bare-metal server infrastructure. They
are the fastest class of proxy and the cheapest per IP, but they are
the easiest to detect.

Best for: large-scale scraping, SEO monitoring, price aggregation,
server testing, anything where stealth is not the priority.

Pricing is per IP per month. Plans:

- 10 IPs — ₦3,000 per month
- 50 IPs — ₦12,000 per month
- 100 IPs — ₦20,000 per month

Available in 20+ regions. HTTP, HTTPS, and SOCKS5 supported.
