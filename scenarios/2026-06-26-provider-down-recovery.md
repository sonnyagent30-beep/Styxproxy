## New Rule Locked This Session

### Dead-IP replacement policy (locked)

```
[IP generated by provider]
   ↓
[Test 1 — curl with 5s timeout]
   ✅ → Deliver
   ❌ → Send back to provider, request replacement
   ↓
[Test 2 — first retry]
   ✅ → Deliver
   ❌ → Second retry
   ↓
[Test 3 — second retry]
   ✅ → Deliver
   ❌ → Third retry
   ↓
[Test 4 — third retry (last attempt)]
   ✅ → Deliver
   ❌ → ALL DEAD → auto-refund
```

**Max attempts: 4 (1 initial + 3 retries).**

**Auto-refund customer message:**

```
⚠️ Sorry, Dan — we hit a technical issue and couldn't generate a working IP for your order.

✅ Your full payment of ₦6,500 has been refunded automatically.
⏰ Refund will land in your account within 24 hours.

We won't try again on this order — please reorder later or try a different product.

Sorry for the inconvenience 🙏
```

**Why 3 retries?** Math: 5% dead rate × 3 retries = 0.0125% chance of all-fail. Near-certain working delivery.

**Cost ROI:** Without retries, 5% of orders = dead IPs delivered = customer complaints + refunds (₦32,500 lost per 100 orders). With retries, ~₦390 in wasted provider costs.
